Return Policy Details

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags attached.

How long does a return take to process?

Returns typically take 3-5 business days to process once received at our warehouse. You'll receive an email confirmation once your return has been processed.

Do you offer free returns?

We offer free returns for customers in the Netherlands. International customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error.

What items are final sale?

Final sale items include clearance items, undergarments, swimwear (if hygiene seal is broken), and customized or personalized items unless they arrive damaged or defective.

What if I receive a damaged or incorrect item?

Please contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos of the damaged/incorrect item and your order number. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund.

Can I exchange an item?

Yes, you can exchange an item for a different size or color, subject to availability. Please note that exchanges may take longer to process than refunds.

International Shipping

Customs & Import Fees

International orders may be subject to customs fees, import duties, and taxes. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in the order total. We recommend checking with your local customs office for more information.

Shipping Restrictions

Some items may be restricted from shipping to certain countries due to local regulations. If we're unable to ship an item to your location, we'll notify you and issue a full refund.

International Returns

International returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs and any customs fees associated with the return. We recommend using a trackable shipping service and purchasing shipping insurance.
