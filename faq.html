Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

General Questions

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the Netherlands and 5-10 business days for international orders. Delivery times may vary depending on your location and any customs processing for international shipments. You'll receive a tracking number once your order has been shipped.

We accept various payment methods including major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Litecoin). All transactions are secure and encrypted for your protection.

Yes, we're committed to ethical and sustainable practices. Our products are sourced from suppliers who adhere to fair labor practices, and we prioritize environmentally friendly materials and processes whenever possible. We also offer a selection of upcycled and second-hand items to reduce waste.

Orders & Shipping

Yes, we offer express shipping options at checkout. Express shipping typically delivers within 1-2 business days for domestic orders and 2-5 business days for international orders. Additional fees apply for express shipping.

Once your order has been shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also log in to your account to view your order status and tracking information.

Yes, we ship worldwide! Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Please note that customers are responsible for any customs fees, import duties, or taxes that may apply in their country.

Returns & Exchanges

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused and unwashed items with tags still attached. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs. For defective or incorrect items, please contact us immediately for assistance. Please see our full Returns & Exchanges page for more details.

To initiate a return, please log in to your account and go to your order history. Select the item(s) you wish to return and follow the instructions. If you checked out as a guest, please contact our customer service team with your order number and the items you'd like to return.

Once we receive your returned items, please allow 3-5 business days for processing. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method. Depending on your bank or credit card company, it may take an additional 3-5 business days for the refund to appear on your statement.

Products

To ensure the longevity of your clothing, we recommend following the care instructions on the garment's label. In general, we recommend washing similar colors together in cold water and air drying when possible to preserve colors and fabric quality. Avoid using bleach or harsh detergents.

Yes, we offer an extended size range for many of our products. Available sizes are listed on each product page. If you need assistance finding the right size, please refer to our size guide or contact our customer service team for personalized recommendations.

Yes, sustainability is at the core of our brand. We use eco-friendly materials, minimize packaging waste, and offer a selection of upcycled and second-hand items. We're committed to reducing our environmental impact and continuously improving our sustainability practices.

Account & Security

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter the email address associated with your account, and we'll send you a link to create a new password. If you don't see the email, please check your spam or junk folder.

We take your privacy and security seriously. We use industry-standard encryption (SSL) to protect your personal information during transmission. We never store full credit card numbers on our servers. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

You can update your account information by logging in to your account and navigating to the "Account Settings" section. From there, you can update your personal information, shipping addresses, payment methods, and communication preferences.
